Privacy Center

At GigHR, your privacy is not just a policy — it’s a promise. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that your data is used responsibly, transparently, and in full compliance with global privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable regulations.

What we collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our services effectively. This includes:

  • • Profile details you provide (name, skills, availability, location)
  • • Gig posts and applications
  • • Communication between users
  • • Technical data (device type, browser, IP address)

How we use your information

Your data is used to match talent with gigs, improve platform functionality, and ensure a secure experience. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for advertising. We use it only to operate, maintain, and improve GigHR.

Your rights and choices

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • • Access and update your profile information
  • • Delete your account and associated data
  • • Request a copy of your stored data
  • • Opt out of non-essential communications

We honor all valid data requests and respond promptly in accordance with applicable laws.

Security and retention

We use secure transport protocols, encrypted storage, and access controls to protect your data. We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill our services or meet legal obligations. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

Third-party services

GigHR may integrate with third-party services (e.g., payment processors, analytics tools) that have their own privacy policies. We vet these providers carefully and ensure they meet our standards for data protection.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Center to reflect changes in law, technology, or platform features. When we do, we’ll notify users clearly and provide a summary of changes.

Contact us

If you have questions about privacy, data usage, or your rights, contact our Privacy Team at privacy@gighr.com.
