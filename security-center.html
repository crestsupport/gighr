Security Center

Security is foundational to GigHr. From the moment you create an account to every interaction you have on the platform, we apply safeguards designed to protect your information, your identity, and your activity. Our commitment is simple: your data is handled responsibly, stored securely, and used only within the boundaries of applicable laws and regulations.

Our security philosophy

GigHr follows a layered security model that combines technical controls, operational discipline, and continuous monitoring. We design our systems to minimize risk, prevent unauthorized access, and ensure that your information remains protected at every stage.

Platform protections

We use industry‑standard security practices to safeguard your data and maintain platform integrity. These include:

  • • **Encrypted data transmission** using secure HTTPS protocols for all interactions.
  • • **Access controls and authentication measures** to prevent unauthorized account access.
  • • **Continuous monitoring** to detect unusual activity or potential threats.
  • • **Regular security reviews** to evaluate system performance and identify improvements.
  • • **Secure infrastructure partners** who meet strict compliance and data protection standards.

How we protect your account

Your account is protected through a combination of platform safeguards and user‑controlled settings. We encourage all users to take advantage of available security features.

  • • **Strong password requirements** to reduce the risk of unauthorized access.
  • • **Session management controls** that help prevent misuse of logged‑in devices.
  • • **Automated alerts** for suspicious login attempts or unusual account behavior.

Data handling and storage

GigHr stores data only for as long as necessary to operate the platform, meet legal obligations, or support legitimate business needs. When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

  • • **Encrypted storage practices** to protect sensitive information.
  • • **Strict access limitations** ensuring only authorized personnel can view operational data.
  • • **Compliance with global privacy laws** including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional regulations.

Third‑party security

GigHr partners with trusted third‑party providers for services such as payments, analytics, and infrastructure. These partners must meet our security standards and comply with all relevant privacy and data protection laws.

  • • **Vendor assessments** to ensure third‑party compliance and reliability.
  • • **Limited data sharing** only for essential operational purposes.
  • • **Contractual safeguards** requiring partners to protect user information.

Your role in security

Security is a shared responsibility. You play an important part in keeping your account and information safe.

  • • **Use strong, unique passwords** and update them regularly.
  • • **Be cautious when sharing personal information** with other users.
  • • **Avoid suspicious links or messages** that may attempt to impersonate GigHr.
  • • **Report unusual activity** so our team can investigate promptly.

Incident response

In the event of a security issue, GigHr follows a structured incident response process designed to minimize impact and restore normal operations quickly.

  • • **Immediate investigation** of reported or detected incidents.
  • • **Containment and mitigation steps** to prevent further impact.
  • • **User notifications** when required by law or when necessary for your protection.
  • • **Post‑incident reviews** to strengthen future safeguards.

Staying informed

We update this Security Center as our practices evolve or as new regulations require. We encourage users to check back periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
